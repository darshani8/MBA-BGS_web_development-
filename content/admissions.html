Who Can Apply

Eligibility Criteria

Academic Qualification

A bachelor's degree in any discipline with a minimum of 50% aggregate (45% for reserved categories) from a recognised university.

Entrance Exams

A valid score in PGCET / CAT / MAT / CMAT / KMAT / ATMA is preferred. Direct admission counselling is also available.

Documents Needed

Marks cards (10th, 12th, degree), transfer & migration certificates, ID proof and passport-size photographs.
