Rooted in values, built for the future

BGSCET MBA is a unit of the BGSKH Education Trust (R.), part of the Sri Adichunchanagiri Shikshana Trust (R.) — an institution with a deep heritage of holistic education guided by the spirit of || jai sri gurudev ||.

Our Parent Institution

Sri Adichunchanagiri Shikshana Trust (R)

BGSCET MBA is nurtured by one of Karnataka's most revered educational and spiritual movements — a legacy of service, learning and character.

Sri Adichunchanagiri Shikshana Trust (R) is the educational wing of the Sri Adichunchanagiri Mahasamsthana Math, a centuries-old centre of spirituality and social welfare. Inspired by the vision of Jagadguru Sri Sri Sri Dr. Balagangadharanatha Mahaswamiji and carried forward by the seers of the Math, the Trust has grown into one of the largest educational networks in the state.

From schools and pre-university colleges to engineering, medical, dental, nursing, pharmacy and management institutions, it serves thousands of students across Karnataka — uniting value-based learning with academic and professional excellence under the timeless ideal of || jai sri gurudev ||. BGSCET and its MBA programme are proud constituents of this enduring legacy.

Our Founder President

The Visionary Who Began It All

“

Education is the greatest gift one can offer society — a lamp that, once lit, dispels the darkness of generations.

Our institutions owe their very existence to the vision of Jagadguru Sri Sri Sri Dr. Balagangadharanatha Mahaswamiji, the 71st pontiff of the Sri Adichunchanagiri Mahasamsthana Math. A seer of rare foresight, he believed that true upliftment begins with access to knowledge — especially for the rural and the underprivileged.

In 1974 he founded the Sri Adichunchanagiri Shikshana Trust (R.), planting the seed of what would grow into one of Karnataka's largest educational movements — schools, pre-university and degree colleges, and institutions of engineering, medicine, dentistry, nursing, pharmacy and management. BGSCET and its MBA programme stand as proud heirs to that enduring legacy.
