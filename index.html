Why BGSCET MBA

The BGSCET Distinction

Everything you need to transform from a graduate into a confident, future-ready business leader.

MBA Boot-Camp

An intensive 18-day launchpad of team challenges, skills assessment and goal-setting that gets you fully MBA-ready before semester one begins.

Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU)

Six courses every semester, 50 hours each — a rigorous academic grounding in core management fundamentals from a respected state university.

Personal Enrichment and Excellence Programme (PEEP)

Three courses per semester through your first year, 30 hours each — building the professional, technical and critical-thinking skills that sharpen your degree.

High Performing & Potential Students (HiPPOS)

Three focused courses in the third semester for career discovery, role-based up-skilling, a foreign language and advanced thinking skills.

Industry 2 Talent · Talent 2 Industry (IT-TI)

30 hours every semester of real-world insight through leadership visits, professors of practice, industry visit and master classes.

Entrepreneurship Development Program (EDP)

End-to-end incubation support for students keen to launch, fund and lead their own ventures from idea to market.

Questions

Frequently Asked

What is the eligibility for the MBA program?

A bachelor's degree in any discipline with a minimum of 50% marks (45% for reserved categories) from a recognised university. A valid score in PGCET / CAT / MAT / CMAT / KMAT is preferred.

When do admissions for 2026–27 close?

Applications for the 2026–27 batch are open now. Early-bird scholarships have limited seats — we recommend applying early. Submit the enquiry form and our team will share the exact timeline.

Are scholarships available?

Yes. We offer merit-based and need-based scholarships, plus full guidance on education loans. Early-bird scholarships of up to ₹1,50,000 are available for the 2026–27 intake.

What kind of placement support do you provide?

Our Corporate Resource Centre provides 100% placement assistance — including resume building, mock interviews, aptitude training, live projects and access to 180+ recruiting partners.

Where is the campus located?

BGSCET is located at CA Site No. 6 & 7, 3rd Main, Pipeline Road, Chord Rd, adj. to Mahalakshmi Metro Station, 2nd Phase, Stage 2, Mahalakshmipuram, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560086 — well connected by metro, bus and rail.
