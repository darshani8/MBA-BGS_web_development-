The 3-Stage Transformation

Your MBA, engineered in three stages.

A deliberately sequenced journey — from a high-intensity bootcamp to a placement-ready finish. Every day is mapped: what you learn, what you build and the real-world exposure you gain along the way.

≈ 24 months end-to-end 10+ professional certifications 1-month live organizational immersion 180+ recruiting partners
01 Reboot18 days · bootcamp 02 ExcelYear 1 · Sem 1 + 2 03 ElevateYear 2 · Sem 3 + 4
01
Reboot

Build the foundation.

18-Day Pre-MBA Bootcamp

An intensive immersion that resets habits and levels the cohort. In just 18 days you build the discipline, communication and analytical base that the next two years stand on.

Day 01 – 10

Onboarding & Foundation Skills

Arrive, benchmark where you stand, then close the gaps that hold graduates back in their first management term.

  • Cohort induction, orientation & goal-mapping
  • Aptitude & skills diagnostic tests
  • Business communication & writing
  • Quantitative aptitude & logical reasoning
  • Spreadsheet & digital fundamentals
Day 11 – 18

Mindset, Presence & Launchpad

Build the confidence of a future leader and step into Semester 1 already speaking the language of business.

  • Public speaking & group discussions
  • Professional grooming & etiquette
  • Team-building & collaboration drills
  • Introduction to the case method & B-school primer
  • Mentor allocation + MBA-readiness review
Outcome
Enter Semester 1 confident, disciplined and truly MBA-ready.
02
Excel

Develop & excel.

Year 1 · Semesters 1 & 2 + breaks

Your first year. Each semester pairs the VTU university syllabus with the PEEP enrichment programme, leadership talks, industry visits, certifications and fests — so you grow academically, professionally and personally at the same time.

Semester 1 · 90 Days

Foundations + First Exposure

You begin with the core university curriculum and immediately layer real-world activities on top.

  • VTU Courses — start with your core university syllabus
  • PEEP — Part 1 — credit-bearing employability & personality enrichment
  • ITTI — 5 leadership talks by industry leaders
  • 2 Industry Visits — see real companies in action
  • 5 Certifications — professional & technical skill badges
  • Varnika — Bangalore's biggest management fest
Semester 1 Break · 10 Days

Recharge

A short, well-earned break to rest and reset before the next semester begins.

Semester 2 · 90 Days

Go Deeper

The same powerful rhythm continues, now with more depth and a community focus.

  • VTU Courses — continue the core university syllabus
  • PEEP — Part 2 — the next level of enrichment & employability
  • ITTI — more leadership talks from industry experts
  • 5 Certifications — another set of professional & technical badges
  • Societal Immersion — a community outreach & social-impact project
After Semester 2 · 1 Month Break · Organizational Study / Internship

Real-World Immersion

The bridge between Excel and Elevate — a full month inside a live organization before you specialise.

  • Organizational Study / Internship — work inside a host company
  • Apply your learning — put classroom frameworks to work on the ground
  • Evaluated Report — submit your study / internship report
Outcome
Strong academics, 10 certifications and your first real-world exposure.
03
Elevate

Become industry-ready.

Year 2 · Semesters 3 & 4 · Placement

Your final year. Semester 3 sharpens your specialization through the VTU curriculum and live projects; Semester 4 turns everything into offers with focused placement training and deep industry exposure.

Semester 3 · 90 Days

Specialize & Apply

The same powerful rhythm continues, now going deep in your chosen field.

  • VTU Courses — continue the university syllabus, now specialized
  • PEEP — Part 3 — the next level of enrichment & employability
  • ITTI — more leadership talks from industry experts
  • 5 Certifications — another set of professional & technical badges
  • Your Track — Finance, Marketing, HR, Logistics or Digital
Semester 4 · The Launch

Growth, Development & Industry Readiness

The final stretch is built around your growth and making you fully industry-ready.

  • Growth & Development — personal and professional growth tracks
  • Industry-Ready — final project & real industry exposure
  • Mock Interviews — intensive interview practice with PEEP & ITTI
  • Convocation — graduate as a confident, industry-ready leader
Outcome
Graduate industry-ready, placed and prepared to lead.
